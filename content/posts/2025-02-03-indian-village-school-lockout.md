title: "Indian Village Elementary School Placed on Lockout After 'Angry Individual' on Campus"
date: 2025-02-03T00:00:00-05:00
author: "WANE 15"
categories: ["Local News"]
---

WANE 15 reported that Indian Village Elementary School was placed on lockout after an angry individual was reported on campus. A lockout restricts access to the building from the outside while normal activities continue inside. The situation was resolved without incident.

*Originally published February 3, 2025 by [WANE 15](https://www.wane.com/top-stories/angry-individual-on-campus-causes-indian-village-elementary-school-to-lockdown/).*
