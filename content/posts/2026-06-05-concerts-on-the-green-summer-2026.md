Aboite Township's free **Concerts on the Green** series continues at **Indian Trails Park** on Wednesday nights. Bring a lawn chair or blanket and enjoy live music from 7–9 PM. Rita's on the Road and Fire Department hot dogs will be available for purchase.

**Remaining 2026 lineup:**

| Date | Band |
|------|------|
| July 22 | MG5 |
| July 29 | Whoa, Man |

All concerts are free and open to the public. In case of rain, the Township will try to move the event to the large park pavilion.

**Indian Trails Park** is located at 5004 Old Trail Road — just a short drive from Indian Village.
