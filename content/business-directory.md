Support your neighbors! The businesses listed below are owned or operated by Indian Village residents.

### Add Your Business

To get your business listed, email [board@indianvillagefw.org](mailto:board@indianvillagefw.org) with the following:

- **Business name** *(required)*
- **Category** *(required)* — pick from the list below, or suggest a new one
- **Short description** *(required)* — a sentence or two about what you do
- **Phone number** *(optional)*
- **Website** *(optional)*
- **Address** *(optional)* — if you want customers to visit

The directory is free and open to any resident-owned or resident-operated business. You don't need to be an IVCA member to be listed.
