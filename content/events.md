Community events, garage sales, and neighborhood gatherings. To submit an event, email [board@indianvillagefw.org](mailto:board@indianvillagefw.org).

**Neighborhood Garage Sales** are held twice a year — the second Saturday in June and the second Saturday in September. Watch for announcements closer to each date.
