Indian Village is located in the Waynedale area of southwest Fort Wayne, Indiana. The neighborhood is bounded roughly by Nuttman Avenue to the north, Bluffton Road to the east, Engle Road to the south, and the Norfolk Southern railroad right-of-way to the west.
