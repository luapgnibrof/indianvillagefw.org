The Fort Wayne Police and Fire departments publish daily activity logs covering all incidents across the city. These are valuable tools for staying aware of what's happening in and around Indian Village.

## Activity Logs

- **[Police Activity Log](https://policeactivitylog.acfw.net/)** — calls for service, incidents, and arrests ([city page](https://www.cityoffortwayne.in.gov/698/Activity-Log))
- **[Fire Activity Log](https://www.cityoffortwayne.in.gov/412/Activity-Log)** — fire and EMS responses

### Filtering for Our Neighborhood

The activity logs cover all of Fort Wayne. To find incidents relevant to Indian Village, look for these street names and landmarks:

- **Indian Village Blvd**, **Pocahontas Trail**, **Hiawatha Blvd**, **Nokomis Place**
- **Lower Huntington Road**, **Bluffton Road**, **Wayne Trace**
- Cross streets and nearby corridors: **Tillman Road**, **Homestead Road**, **Decatur Road**

You can typically use your browser's search (Ctrl+F / Cmd+F) to quickly find these names in the log.
